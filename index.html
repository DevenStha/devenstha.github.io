Why I gave up on Linux gaming

Linux is cool and all but I have to give up on daily driving due to following reasons:

  • Microstutters
  • Windows Media Foundation videos [ Biggest Reason ]
  • Paranoia because I didn't want to miss out on any cutscene because I happened to play a game with media foundation and it didn't play or straight up crashed souring my mood
  • I have a fucking NVIDIA GPU, and the performance is worse compared to windows
  • Non gaming related but goddamn when I open my laptop from sleep, a lot of the times it just freezes after I enter password and try to get back Btw, This happened in KDE Plasma upto version 6.3 (Wayland) in my Hybrid Intel + Nvidia Laptop
  • And no, freeze after sleep happens even if I didn't run any updates and forgot to restart whatsoever
  • Also sometimes my laptop just randomly freezes and no key works and I am forced to force restart my laptop, it has never happened to me in Windows

My laptop spec:

  • CPU

  • RAM

  • GPU
    - NVIDIA Geforce RTX 2050

    - EndeavourOS and CachyOS

So, that why I gave up on Linux and came back to Windows 10 IoT Enterprise LTSC 2021
